Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa, also known as the city of Living God, will offer you an unforgettable experience by visiting some of its impressive historical landmarks. Additionally, it has also been an appealing destination for sun worshippers and those seeking enjoyment. Thanks to the small, stunning islands, many white sand beaches make a wonderful place, namely Honda Bay. This is a perfect spot for swimming and picnicking while traveling to Puerto Princesa. Plus, you shouldn’t miss a chance to discover a river that lies underground for more than 8 kilometres, which is also one of the New 7 Wonders of the World. Puerto Princesa is the capital city of Palawan’s island province. This capital city has been acclaimed as one of the greenest & cleanest cities in this country. Puerto Princesa is home to an abundance of impressive attractions, ranging from historical landmarks, beaches to wildlife reserves. To get around the city, you can either take a multi-cab or a tricycle, which are the most common means in Puerto Princesa
